It Is

“San Roque is San Roque. A quaint town in California.”

“I’ve never seen it on the maps.”

Which isn’t unusual for California, there’s a lot of random towns around here. But usually people always mention a small town once to you in your life. Such as having to pass through one to get to another, or your friend saying they’re moving out to the most random town in the state. California is just odd in general. Small towns, weird people, sometimes beautiful landscape…or dry dirt that’s scorched and gross.

California is California. San Roque is San Roque.
