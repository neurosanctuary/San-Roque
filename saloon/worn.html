Worn and Torn

The photo is dear to me. I hold it close to my heart. Rub on it when I need something of comfort. Something to remind me of what I am doing. Something that tells me to keep on going. I look at its folds as if it is a piece of history. Bits of my search. It shows off that I’ve been doing something. That I haven’t just been sitting around in misery.

My little piece of home.
