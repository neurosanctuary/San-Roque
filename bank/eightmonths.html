Eight Months

Eight months affects you greatly. I had everything lined up for what I wanted to do. Everything for myself and with you. But it all just crumbled when you left, I didn’t know what to do afterwards. I do though. I do know what I want to do. But I can’t imagine it without you. Please just look at the photo, please. Tell me yes or no. Just something so I can have a direct idea in my mind.

I don’t want a maybe, because a maybe is unknowledgeable. I want an answer that can guide me.
