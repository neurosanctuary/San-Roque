Tomorrow

I don’t know if I should consider listening, I didn’t say much.

I wouldn’t call it comforting either, we were total strangers.

But it was nice, to sit a bit in similar thoughts.

“I have to get going, it was nice…”

“Moonpie.”

“Tumbleweed.”

“Always a pleasure to meet another worrywart.”

“And maybe we’ll both try to not think so hard.”

“No harm in it!”

It’s a small laugh, as Moonpie reaches through the window to grab my paw. A little pat that shows things will work out.

I say goodbye and it looks like my thoughts have urged me to look around just a bit more.
