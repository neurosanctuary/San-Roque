Look

“Just a yes or no answer please. Just look at it one more time.”

Something as simple as asking about a photo felt so exhausting. I wanted to scream and cry. I wanted to bash my head into the bars before me. I felt as if every moment was of some importance yet also it didn’t matter at all.

“No. If that’s all, can you please leave.”

“I could stay here all I want, your sign still says you're open.”

“I never move the sign, no one comes in here anyways. I have better things to do than sit around waiting for a customer.”

“You pushed a potential customer out.”

“I’ve never seen you around before, so I knew you didn’t live out here nor move here.”

His logic for pushing me out right away made no sense. Was he anxious? Was he lazy? Did he just not want to work?

“You have a face that really shows when you’re thinking too hard about something.”
